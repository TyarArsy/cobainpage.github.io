Tribute to Kurt Cobain

"A talented yet troubled grunge performer, Kurt Cobain was the frontman for Nirvana and became a rock legend in the 1990s"

Timeline of Cobain's Life

  • 1967 - Born in Aberdeen, Washington, U.S.
  • 1971 - At 4 years old he started playing the piano and singing.
  • 1976 - His parents get divorced which led him to change his personality, becoming defiant and withdrawn.
  • 1985 - He forms a band "Fecal Matter" with band members from The Melvins.
  • 1986 - Moves into an apartment, paying rent while working at coastal resort north of Aberdeen.
  • 1987 - Nirvana is formed after friend Krist Novoselic agrees to join him.
  • 1989 - Nirvana releases their first album with drummer Chad Channing, called "Bleach".
  • 1991 - A second album "Nevermind" was released which brought Nirvana into the mainstream with the single "Smells Like Teen Spirit". Dave Grohl was featured on this album and remained as the last drummer.
  • 1992 - In February he married "Hole" singer and guitarist Courtney Love. The couple's only child, Frances Bean Cobain, was born in August.
  • 1993 - Nirvana releases their final studio album, "In Utero".
  • 1994 - In April he was found in his apartment with a gunshot wound to his head, which was believed to be self-inflicted.
